MASP - Museu de Arte de S達o Paulo

For this assignment, we were divided into groups of 3 and were supposed to research about an already existing building from a given list. We had to analyze its structure and understand how it was built in order to replicate a sectional model of it. My group chose the S達o Paulo Museum of Art located in S達o Paulo, Brazil.

Information was gathered using resources such as online papers, websites and ebooks. Two sectional drawings were used as guide, and a drawing was made as one of the required deliverables.

After all information was gathered and schematic drawings and sketches were made, a sectional model at 1/4"= 1'0" scale was built. An acrylic sheet was placed at the right end so that the model could stand. The purpose of using the acrylic as a material was to maximize visibility.
